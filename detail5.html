企业优惠券系统 Enterprise Token System

企业优惠券系统 系统为企业内部用户提供优惠券申请、审批渠道，用户可根据流程申请在线商城的优惠券，权限高的用户可对申请进行审批。因涉及的人数、产品种类、组织架构比较多，申请和审批流程比较复杂，设计难点是如何让用户在碎片时间内填写好所需信息，做了以下调整： 设计要点： 1. 新版logo
2. 申请表分为多个步骤，信息归类清晰，避免手动填写信息
3. 以合适的方式加入相关引导
Enterprise Token System The system helps police department evaluate the areas under the jurisdiction of the subordinate police stations from five latitudes (according to the information and score uploaded by the police officers), in order to understand the work of the grassroots police officers, checks hidden dangers and takes timely actions. Design Keypoints: 1. Conduct in-depth research on business scenarios to determine function layout and operational logic
2. Designed for user groups with different permissions
3. Display the data designed for the mobile phone screen and operate the filtering conditions flexibly
