商业车辆数据分析系统 Vehicle Links

商业车辆数据分析系统 对商业车辆的行径轨迹、司机操作、设备情况、路段种类、行驶时间等数据进行呈现和分析，该设计为大屏的展示部分，决策者查看关键数据统计并及时提出解决方案。 设计要点： 1. 设计低保真UI供各方讨论和研究
2. 参与各方讨论并决定数据的呈现方式
3. 设计UI并在进行4K屏幕调试
Vehicle Links A data analysis system for commercial car companies to get track of their cars, staff and relevant equipment. Dashboard pages are designed to present critical data to help management with their decision making.  Design Process: 1. Draw low definition wireframe for discussion 
2. Determine how the data is presented
3. Design UIs and make sure they are displayed properly on 4K screens
